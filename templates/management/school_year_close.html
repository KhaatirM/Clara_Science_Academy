This runs end-of-year report cards (official, Q1–Q4, with attendance and comments) for every current student who has an active enrollment in the year you select, then archives that year: assignments for those classes are set to Inactive (Voided items stay Voided), enrollments are deactivated, classes are marked inactive, and the school year is marked inactive.
