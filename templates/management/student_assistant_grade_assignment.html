Use this guide to correctly record grades so the teacher doesn't have to double-check.

Submitted?

Not submitted — The student did not turn in the assignment. Leave score blank or enter 0. The teacher will know not to look for it.

Yes (paper/in-person) — The student turned in a physical paper or handed it in during class.

Yes (online) — The student submitted digitally through the app.

Score
