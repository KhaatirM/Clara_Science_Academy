Group assignments: what to expect

These scenarios help avoid surprises when using group assignments.

  • 1
    Due date & close date
    When the due date or close date passes, the assignment becomes Inactive. Students can no longer submit, and it no longer appears as active for you or for students.
  • 2
    Deleting a group
    If you (or an administrator) delete a group, existing grades and submissions for that group are kept. You can still view and edit those grades on the assignment’s Grade page under “Students from deleted group”. You do not need to re-enter grades.
  • 3
    Changing which groups are selected
    When you edit an assignment and change which groups are included, existing grades and submissions are not removed. Students in groups you remove will still have their grades; you can view and edit them under “Students from deleted group” on the Grade page.
  • 4
    Students in removed or deleted groups
    Those students can no longer submit for this assignment, but they keep any grade already entered and can still see that assignment and grade on their dashboard.
