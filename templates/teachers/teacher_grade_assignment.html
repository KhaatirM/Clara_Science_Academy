Grading Fields Guide

Use this guide to correctly record grades and submission status.

Submission Status

Not Submitted — Student did not turn in the assignment. No grade needed unless giving a zero.

Submitted (Paper/In-Person) — Student handed in a physical paper or turned it in during class.

Submitted (Online) — Student submitted digitally through the app.

Submission Notes

Optional notes such as "Turned in late," "Resubmitted," or "Excused absence."

Points Earned

Points the student earned out of the total. If extra credit is enabled for this assignment, you can enter points above the base total up to the configured EC maximum. Enter 0 for no credit or not submitted.

Feedback

Optional comment visible to the student. Add constructive feedback to help them improve.
